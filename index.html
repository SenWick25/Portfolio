01. About Me

I'm a passionate undergraduate pursuing dual degrees in Computer Science and Mechatronics Engineering, driven by a lifelong fascination with "How things actually work!?". This innate curiosity has grown into a deep passion for technological automation, robotics, and intelligent systems, shaping both my academic journey and career aspirations.

As an aspiring Systems Analyst, I specialize in translating complex user requirements into comprehensive system designs using UML diagrams and OOP principles. I believe I have the ability to analyze and break down complex systems through diagrams such as Use Case, State, Activity, Sequence, and Class Diagrams. With a background in both software and hardware, I bring a well-rounded perspective to system development and design.

My ongoing internship at Ceylon Busicut Limited (CBL) is giving me hands-on experience with PLC programming, HMI interfaces, and industrial automation systems. This practical application has significantly helped me bridge theoretical knowledge with practical implementation.

Beyond my technological and academic pursuits, I have always shown keen interest in extracurricular activities and sports. I believe various experiences and opportunities have cultivated me into a strong, collaborative, and well-rounded leadership personality.
